Why This Is Different

Most bond tools show one variable. This system combines multiple signal layers into a single warning framework, making it easier to spot when conditions are merely restrictive versus genuinely deteriorating.

What It Is Designed For

The Early Warning System is built for macro monitoring, scenario analysis and rapid interpretation. It is meant to simplify complexity, not replace detailed market research.

Methodology

The warning system combines six simplified signal blocks: curve pressure, real yield pressure, cross-market stress, liquidity stress, credit stress and safe-haven demand.

These blocks are weighted into a single Early Warning Score and then translated into a warning level: Stable, Watch, Elevated, High Risk or Critical.

This BondStats model is for informational and educational purposes only. It is a simplified analytical framework, not an official forecast, regulatory measure or investment recommendation.
